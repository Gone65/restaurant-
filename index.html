About Us

Our Story

Josi Restaurant was founded with a passion for bringing authentic Habeshan cuisine to your table. Our journey began with a dream to share the rich culinary traditions passed down through generations.

Every dish at Josi is carefully prepared using traditional methods and the finest ingredients. We believe that food is more than nourishment—it's a celebration of culture, heritage, and community.

Our expert chefs combine years of experience with a deep respect for traditional Habeshan cooking, ensuring that every meal is an unforgettable experience. We welcome you to join our family and taste the difference that authenticity and passion make.
